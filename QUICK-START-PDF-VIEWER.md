## FAQ

### Q: Why can't I see the next page button?
**A**: It's been replaced with continuous scrolling! Just scroll down to see the next page.

### Q: Can I zoom to any percentage?
**A**: Between 50-200%. Type directly in the zoom field for any value in this range.

### Q: Does fullscreen work on mobile?
**A**: Yes! Fullscreen works on most modern mobile devices. May be limited on some older devices.

### Q: Where's the search feature?
**A**: Search will be added in a future update. You can use browser search (Ctrl+F) for text in the current view.

### Q: Can I print the PDF?
**A**: Yes! Use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P) or the download button.

### Q: What if the PDF won't load?
**A**: Click "Open in New Tab" button to try opening it directly. If it works there, the issue is with the viewer. Report it!
